About

The Storyteller
Behind the Frame

"Every story I write is a wound I'm trying to understand — and a world I'm inviting others into."

Tankith Akhilesh is a Telugu screenwriter currently working with Chai Bisket / Chai Shots as a Writer and AI Dubbing Language Expert. With a passion for storytelling rooted in Telugu cinema's raw emotional core, he is now channeling years of writing craft into his first steps as a feature film director.

Inspired by the visceral intensity of Puri Jagannath, the dark psychological depth of Sandeep Reddy Vanga, and the suspense mastery of Alfred Hitchcock, Tankith brings a distinctive voice to every story he tells — stories that refuse to be forgotten.
